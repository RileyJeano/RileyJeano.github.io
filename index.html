About Me

Hi! I'm Riley.

Current Lead Game Designer at AfroDuck Studios.


By day I'm a software developer.
By night I'm a game designer.
By later in the night I'm a very tired artist.

I'm a recent college graduate with 4+ years coding experience. Art and Technology major; the union of a Bachelors of Fine Arts and CS. Check out some of my work on the art website tab at the top!

I currently work as the lead game designer at the startup AfroDuck Studios. I also work on the programming, writing, and art teams as well.

When I'm not programming I'm developing video games, making animations, printmaking, painting, reading, writing, or swimming. I've been told I do too much. But life is too short to sit still!
